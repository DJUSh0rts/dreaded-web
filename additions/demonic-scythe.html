    Demonic Scythe

    The Demonic Scythe is a powerful weapon with effects based on different crops you grow.

    Details

    • The Demonic Scythe is a powerful weapon built around crop-based effects.
    • Its effects change depending on the crops you use.

    Tips

    • Use the Demonic Scythe when you want a hard-hitting weapon that works best in controlled fights.
    • Keep a food item ready in your offhand to maximize the weapon's unique bonuses.
