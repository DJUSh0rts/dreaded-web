    Hay Dummy

    The Hay Dummy is a sturdy training target for testing powerful weapons without wasting time on real mobs.

    How It Works

    • Place a carved pumpkin on top of a hay bale to summon the Hay Dummy.
    • The dummy has 40 health and displays its current health above its head.
    • After it stops taking player damage, it heals back toward full health over time.
    • If a nearby player holds wheat, the dummy turns to look at them.

    Tips

    • Use it to compare weapon damage, enchantments, and combat setups in a controlled spot.
    • Hold wheat when you want the dummy to face you for cleaner tests and screenshots.
    • Give it a little time between heavy hits if you want to watch the healing behavior.
