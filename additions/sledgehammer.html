    Sledgehammer

    The Sledgehammer is a powerful knockback weapon for sending enemies flying.

    Details

    • The Sledgehammer hits hard and sends enemies flying.
    • It hits hard and pushes targets back.

    Tips

    • Use the Sledgehammer to knock back mobs or to control chokepoints.
    • Avoid using it in fast mob swarms unless you have room to time your attacks.
