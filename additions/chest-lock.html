    Chest Lock

    A lock system for protecting storage containers with easy management.

    How It Works

    • Shift + Right-click on a chest to open the gui or do /cl gui. You can add trusted players, passwords, and stop hopper interaction.
    • Other players will be locked out and shown a chat saying its your chest.
    • This works for chests, barrels and furnaces.

    Commands

    Command Use
    /cl gui Command for opening gui for chest you are looking at.
    /cl trust <player> Add a trusted player to the chest you are looking at.
    /cl untrust <player> Remove a trusted player from the chest you are looking at.
    /cl password <01234> Attempt to access protected chest you are looking at with password.
