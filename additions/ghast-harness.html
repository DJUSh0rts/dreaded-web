    Ghast Harnesses & Motus

    Harnesses are wearable items for ghasts and similar entities. The Motus enchantment increases flying speed when applied to harnesses.

    Motus Enchantment

    • Description: Motus — increases flying speed when on harnesses.
    • Max level: 3
    • Cost: base 5, +10 per level above first. Anvil cost: 7
    • Applies to: Harness items (tag: #minecraft:harnesses)
    • Effect: Adds to the flying_speed attribute: base +0.025, +0.015 per level above first.

    Tips

    • Apply Motus to harnesses you intend to use for mobility boosts.
    • Use dyed harnesses to mark different ghasts or mounts visually.
