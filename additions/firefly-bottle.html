    Firefly Bottle

    The Firefly Bottle creates a temporary light source that helps you explore dark areas.

    Details

    • The Firefly Bottle creates a short-lived light source to help in dark areas.
    • It provides light for a short time before fading.

    Tips

    • Use Firefly Bottles before entering caves or strongholds to avoid torches and keep the mood dark.
    • The summoned light source makes it easier to track mobs and treasure.
