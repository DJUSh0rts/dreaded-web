    Group Advancements

    Community goals that reward the whole server when they are completed.

    How it works

    • These advancements track shared progress across all players.
    • They include goals for mob kills and villager trades.
    • When the server reaches a milestone, the advancement unlocks for everyone.

    Goals

    • Executioner series: kill mobs together, from 100 up to 100000 kills.
    • Monopoly series: trade with villagers together, from 50 up to 100000 trades.

    Why it matters

    • Group Advancements give the server a shared objective and reward teamwork.
    • They are designed to keep progress feeling meaningful and connected for everyone.
