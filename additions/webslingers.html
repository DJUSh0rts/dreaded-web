    Webslingers

    Spiders now fire sticky web projectiles at players, adding a dangerous new attack pattern.

    How It Works

    • Spiders will shoot sticky web projectiles toward players.
    • The web can slow you down and make escaping a fight much harder.
    • This behavior happens naturally in combat.

    Tips

    • Keep moving in spider encounters to avoid getting pinned by web shots.
    • Use ranged attacks or sprint away to reduce the chance of being hit.
