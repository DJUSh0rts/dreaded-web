    Echo Disenchantment

    Echo Disenchantment lets you remove and save enchantments from your tools.

    Details

    • The Echo Book lets you remove enchantments and store them for later use.
    • Use it to remove enchantments and keep them for later.

    Tips

    • Save Echo Books for special gear that you want to transform rather than normal enchantment removal.
    • These books are best used when you have high-value enchanted items and want a controlled change.
