    Shrink Mushroom

    Shrink Mushrooms let you fit through cracks and jump higher.

    Details

    • Shrink Mushrooms let you become smaller to fit through tight spaces.
    • It shrinks you so you can access tight spaces and leap farther.

    Tips

    • Use Shrink Mushroom before dropping from high terrain to survive without relying on water or ladders.
    • Watch your health closely while shrunk, since max health is reduced.
